About Me

The Person Behind the Work

Beyond the research — who I actually am.

🏙️
From Sylhet, Bangladesh
Born and raised in Sylhet — a city famous for tea gardens, rivers, and Sufi shrines. It's where I learned to think slowly and move fast.
📚
Still in High School
I'm a Class XI student balancing HSC prep, independent research, and running Dynexly — all at the same time. No shortcuts, no breaks.
🌙
A Late Night Person
Most of my best ideas — and worst bugs — happen after 12am. There's something about the quiet that makes things clearer.
"I'm building things that matter before the world has given me permission to. The papers, the code, the organization — none of it was asked for. That's the point." — Prosun, on why he started Dynexly at 17
🔭
Genuinely Obsessed with Physics-Informed Machine Learning
Not because it's useful for ML. Because the universe being mathematical is the most interesting fact about existence. I'd study physics even if ML didn't exist.
🌏
Dreaming Global, Building Local
I want to study at MIT or Stanford, but the problem I'm solving — power instability in Bangladeshi hospitals — is home. The two aren't separate.
